  • Quasar

I built this app as a group project together with Nicole. The idea (which came from Nicole) was to create an application to plan a vegetable garden over the course of each year. In order to achieve this the app needed the functionality to select plant varieties, add plant varieties to the plant beds of the garden and to adjust the placement of the plants.

  • create and save garden plans
  • view and select available plant varieties
  • view information about available plant species and varieties
  • ajust garden plans both during the initital planning phase as well as over the course of each year
  • responsive design
